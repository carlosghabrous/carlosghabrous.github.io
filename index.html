Who I am

Senior Software Engineer

Carlos would love to build physical objects with his own hands, but unfortunately he is only reasonably good at writing software. He does not like introducing himself much using the 3ed person, and neither does he like his arms crossed in resume photos. Carlos loves listening to music and playing drums, suffering in triathlons of all distances, learning languages, reading and cooking with a cold beer in his hand. Not at the same time though. He is very much concerned about climate change, and tries to educate his son and daughter into respect for others and the planet. Even if at some point life on Mars becomes a real thing.

My background is in telecommunication engineering, and I consider myself a self taught software engineer who tries to beat the impostor syndrome by keeping on learning new things and by applying my knowledge into cool projects. When I am working I spend most of my time trying to figure out why my software doesn't work as I expect, talking to people to reach agreements and trying to help others.
